Upload data for photogrammetry processing



After you log in, click on the card that shows your project -






Click on Upload data button -






A pop up will appear. To upload drone images for photogrammetry processing click on the button on the left side -






Next, Drag and Drop the drone images that need to be processed -






After that, click on Upload -





Upon Clicking on upload, the images start getting uploaded.
The map also shows you the location of each of the images from the drone.
This helps you make sure that you have uploaded all the images that you need.





Once the images are uploaded, click on 'Back to Main' -





Next, Click on 'Click to Start Processing'





The data processing will start indicated by the rotating gear -





After the data is processed, you can click on the map to visualize the generated maps.




Upload data for Map Visualization



After you log in, click on the card that shows your project -






Click on Upload data button -






A pop up will appear. To upload drone images for map visualization, click on the button on the right side -






Next, Click on choose and select the file for each type -






After that, click on Upload -





Upon Clicking on upload, the images start getting uploaded.
On successful uploads, the icon turns green against each file type.





Once all the files are uploaded, click on 'Create Map for visualization' -






The map visualization process will start indicated by the rotating gear -





After the process is completed, you can click on the map to visualize the generated maps.
